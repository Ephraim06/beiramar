Location

Blouberg - Cape Town, is renowned for its picturesque views of Table Mountain.

Located in Coral Road, Bloubergstrand. Our development offers proximity to popular destinations such as the iconic Blouberg Beach, vibrant Eden on the Bay, and convenient shopping at Table Bay Shopping Centre and Bayside Mall. Nature enthusiasts will appreciate the nearby Rietvlei Wetland Reserve.

Indulge in luxury with rooftop recreational facilities, high-end finishes, and smart locks for secure and convenient access from anywhere in the world.

Beachside Bites: Culinary Adventures on Blouberg Beachfront

Blouberg Beachfront offers a variety of dining options ranging from casual eateries to upscale restaurants. You can enjoy fresh seafood dishes, international cuisine, beachside cafes and local South African specialities. Some popular choices include seafood platters, sushi, burgers, pizzas and traditional South African braai (barbecue). Additionally, you'll find coffee shops, ice cream parlours, and bakeries offering delicious treats and refreshing beverages to enjoy while taking in the stunning beach views.
